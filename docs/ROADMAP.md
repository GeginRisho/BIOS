# BIOS Future Development Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic engineering goals for the future extension of the BIOS platform.

---

## 1. Feature Expansion Timeline

### Q3 2026: Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)
- **Objective:** Train Graph Convolutional Networks (GCNs) directly on Neo4j relations to predict competitor market shares automatically.
- **Goal:** Predict supplier bankruptcy risk propagation across secondary supply tiers.

### Q4 2026: Zero-Knowledge twin ownership verification
- **Objective:** Secure business claims using ZK-Proof structures.
- **Goal:** Allow corporate owners to verify identity without sharing sensitive regulatory documents in plaintext.

### Q1 2027: Real-Time planetary satellite ingestion
- **Objective:** Integrate high-frequency satellite feeds using computer vision models (YOLO).
- **Goal:** Estimate retail store density by counting parking lot vehicles dynamically.

```
┌───────────────────────────┐      ┌───────────────────────────┐
```

- **Scale targets:** Ingest 100M+ businesses digital twins.
